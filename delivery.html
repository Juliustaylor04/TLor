Step by Step

How Delivery Works

From order to your doorstep — simple and transparent

Place Your Order

Browse our collections, add items to cart, fill in your name, phone number, and delivery address, then hit "Send Order via WhatsApp." That's it — your order lands straight in our inbox.

⚡ Takes 2 minutes

We Call to Confirm

We'll review your order and call you to confirm availability, your delivery fee based on your location, and an estimated arrival time. We never send anything without you knowing the full cost first.

📞 Within 30 minutes

We Pack & Dispatch

Once you confirm, we pack your order carefully to make sure everything arrives safely. Then it's handed to our rider who will call you 20–30 minutes before arriving.

🛵 On the way

Receive & Pay

When the rider arrives, open the package and inspect your items. Once you're satisfied, pay the total — product price plus delivery fee — in cash. No hidden charges, no surprises.

💵 Cash on delivery
⚠️

Before You Order

Please make sure your phone number is correct and reachable — we will call to confirm every order. If we can't reach you within 2 hours, the order may be placed on hold. For store pickup, bring a valid ID matching the name on your order. All sales are final once payment is made and package is accepted.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to the things people ask most

How much is delivery?

▼
The delivery fee depends on your location. When you place an order, we'll call you to confirm your address and tell you the exact delivery fee before we send anything out. You'll always know the full cost upfront.

Do I pay before delivery?

▼
No. You pay cash on delivery — both the product price and the delivery fee together when the rider arrives. We don't ask for mobile money or bank transfer upfront.

How long does delivery take?

▼
Most orders within Freetown are delivered the same day if placed early enough. We'll give you an estimated time when we call to confirm. Orders placed late in the day may arrive the next day.

What if I'm not home when the rider comes?

▼
The rider will call you before arriving. If you're not available, we can reschedule for another time or day at no extra cost. If we can't reach you after multiple attempts, the order will be held for up to 1 hours before being cancelled.

Will my perfume arrive safely?

▼
Yes. We handle every order with care to make sure your perfume arrives in perfect condition. Glass bottles are handled properly and packed securely. If anything arrives damaged, contact us immediately and we'll sort it out.

Can I return or exchange after delivery?

▼
Because perfumes are personal and hygiene-sensitive, we don't accept returns once the package is opened and payment is made. However, if the wrong item was delivered or something arrived damaged, contact us immediately on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out.

Do you deliver outside Freetown?

▼
Yes, we can arrange delivery to other parts of Sierra Leone. Delivery fees and times will vary and will be confirmed over the phone before we dispatch. Just include your full address when ordering and we'll work it out.

Can someone else receive my order?

▼
Yes, as long as the person has the cash ready and can confirm the order details (name and phone number). Just let us know when we call to confirm that someone else will be receiving it.
